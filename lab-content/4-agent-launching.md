## Launching an Agent

On the top right of the build screen, you will see a 'Launch Agent' Button. This lets you launch your agent on the [Lyzr Marketplace](https://studio.lyzr.ai/agent-marketplace) with a Chat style interface that you can share with others. 

Feel free to check out other applications built on Lyzr [Here](https://studio.lyzr.ai/agent-marketplace)
